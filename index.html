Mixedstar

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

The short version

Mixedstar does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Data collection

Mixedstar does not collect any personal information. The app:

  • Does not require an account or sign-in
  • Does not use analytics or tracking
  • Does not contain advertising
  • Does not connect to any server
  • Does not access the internet

Your content

Videos, frames, drawings, and any other content you create in Mixedstar are stored locally on your device. Mixedstar does not upload, share, or transmit your content anywhere.

When you use system features like Share or Print, your content is handled by iOS — not by Mixedstar.

Photos & camera access

Mixedstar may request access to your photo library to import videos and save exported content. This access is used solely to read and write files you choose. No data leaves your device through the app.

Third-party services

Mixedstar does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children's privacy

Mixedstar does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Since the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
